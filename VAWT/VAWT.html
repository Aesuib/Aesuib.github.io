The normal wind turbine power generator must be built against the main wind direction of a location, As all of us aircraft pilot know, different city has different wind rose chart, it is drawn by a meteorologist with historical data statistics, and the chart is provided to airport designer, help to decide the orientation of the runway.

A wind turbine is fixed after built, just like a runway. So, people invented VAWT, a Vertical Axis Wind Turbine， which is friendly to all directions of the wind.

Any wind will drive it, and generate electricity. As I saw it near my home, I must have a try, modeling it in SimplePlanes, and I did it!

Vertical Axis Wind Turbine

operations:

It is fixed by default, decrease wind to zero first, and press 1 to activate 1, this will ignite the turbine with a speed very slowly. Then increase the wind little by little, just like what I do in the video recorded. it will accelerate very slowly but will achieve a very fast rotating speed in the final, just wait patiently, and after that, enjoy yourself!
