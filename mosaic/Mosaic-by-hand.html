How to mosaic a photo by hand

As you have read:

三十六年弹指一挥间

I have "TOP GUN II" an essay. After that, many friends asked me, what is this?

This is a snapshot of the movie, it provokes our mother very very much, so, that I mosaiced it.

To mosaic it by hand, you should select a presentation editor you like, Micro$aft PowerPoint, ONLYOFFICE, WPS, etc. I selected ONLYOFFICE, plug the snapshot into a slide first,

To be friendly to all, I didn't store that snapshot on my computer, as it is widespread on the web, to demonstrate the handwork, I select another snapshot very cool.

But why? why should I mosaic a photo by hand? Gimp, PhotoShop, and a lot of software provide the function of mosaicing, easy to use! I do not believe in them, they are developed by people, product managers design the feature of the software, scientists develop the algorithm behind the mosaics, and coders program in C++, make the software alive, so, all these are known to people, scientists can develop an algorithm to decode mosaic to the original photo, and coders can write code to get the original photo from a mosaiced photo, and the final boss behind the software companies, such as super power can get what is really in our minds.

So, as you know, let all the photo editing softwares go, I select to hand draw mosaic in presentations, no one can see what the original photo is

Plug in a rectangle, the GUI of ONLYOFFICE is very like Micro$aft PowerPoint, it is good news.

Here is a little difference between ONLYOFFICE and PowerPoint. The dynamic menu of content is on the ribbon menu bar in PowerPoint, but the menu of content is on the right side button bar in ONLYOFFICE. But the real operations they provide are exactly the same. Set the border none, and set the filling gradient color. Select the color freely, delete gradient controlling points to only 2 controlling points left, and drag them to the left and right ends of the gradient properties slider bar. Set one point the color you select and the other white, set the 2 points transparent, and adjust the transparency as your will. The first mosaic of yours is ok now, duplicate it one by one, adjust the orientation, the size, and the position freely to simulate many different mosaics.

Till the photo be mosaiced all.

Check carefully, and make sure to hide all the sensitive details in the original photo, now, the most important step is,

Move the mouse cursor to the center of the photo, then do not move the mouse, just right click, in the menu pops up, select "Send to the bottom", right-click again to pop up the menu for the second mosaic rectangle here, and send it to bottom too, repeat, till the original photo center appeared,

Now, click this position, the original photo will be selected, and then, use all your power with your right hand, smash down the "delete" button on your keyboard

Finally, Now, the final work is finished! All the nasty points are mosaicked! what a beautiful mosaic picture! No one can be annoyed by the original snapshot of a banned movie now! Next, export the final slide as a .png picture file.
